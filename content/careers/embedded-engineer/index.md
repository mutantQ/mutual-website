**We make digital content provably authentic at the hardware level.**

AI-generated media is indistinguishable from reality. Software detection doesn't work. At mutual, we're building cryptographic signing directly into camera hardware, so content is authenticated the moment it's captured.

Corporate introduction: [Introducing mutual](/blog/introducing-mutual/)

## The Role

We need someone to own the firmware stack: Linux bring-up to TrustZone secure world code to camera pipeline integration.

You'll work closely with our hardware engineer on the reference implementation, and eventually help scale this to production devices.

## What You'll Build

- Embedded Linux (Buildroot/Yocto) for vision SoCs
- OP-TEE trusted applications for real-time video signing
- Camera capture and encoding pipeline
- Secure boot chain implementation

## Requirements

- Experience with embedded Linux development
- Comfortable reading datasheets and schematics
- Can work in Seoul (SNU campus) or hybrid

## Nice to Have

- ARM TrustZone / OP-TEE experience
- Camera pipeline work (V4L2, ISP tuning)
- Experience with Rockchip or similar vision SoCs

## What We Offer

- Competitive equity for early team member
- Interesting technical problem
- Small team, high ownership
